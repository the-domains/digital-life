IPhone 7 Drops Headphone Jack and Will Be Waterproof

The most controversial iPhone 7 feature might already have been revealed

Apple will definitely be removing the headphone port in its next major iPhone release, according to a report. The company is set to swap the traditional headphone jack and instead send sound through the lightning port that at the moment is mostly used for charging, according to a report in Fast Company.
